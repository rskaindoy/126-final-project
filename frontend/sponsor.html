What does it mean to be a sPAWnsor?

🐾 Be Their Lifeline—One Month at a Time! 🐾

All around the university, there are countless stray cats and dogs facing hunger, thirst, sickness, and uncertainty every single day. With no one to care for them, they’re left hoping for kindness. That’s where you can step in! 💖 As a monthly sponsor, you can change their world. 🌎✨

Your monthly support helps provide:

🩺 Nexgard Spectra Medication
Deworming and anti-tick treatments for our campus animals—plus an emergency fund ready to cover urgent needs! 🩹🚑 The Nexgard Spectra is worth at least Php 800.00

Also, if there are extra funds left, we will provide additional:

🍲 Food & Clean Water
Full bellies and fresh drinking water every day mean healthier, happier pets who can play 🐾💧 No more scavenging for scraps or drinking from dirty puddles!

🎁 Comfort & Love
Collars, treats, cozy bowls for food and water—these simple things make them feel safe and loved. 🐕🎀🐈

💡 Getting started is easy! There are flexible sponsorship levels, so you can give in a way that works for you. Whether you choose to give a little or a lot, it all adds up to a huge difference! 🌟

💛 Your kindness brings hope. Together, we can build a compassionate community where no animal within the campus is forgotten. Be the reason their tails wag and their hearts heal!

Thank you for making their world a better place—one paw at a time! 🐶🐱💕
